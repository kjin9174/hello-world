Affiliate Program!

With our Automatic Affiliate Program your Public Posts not only become a Viral Marketing Source, they also become money generators for you! When somebody that has never visited the site before clicks on your Post that went to another Social Site, and that person makes any Purchase from the site, you earn a Lifetime Commission of 10% on all their purchases. Paid on the 3rd day of the month following the purchase. All Members whether Free or Paid are automatically Affiliates. When a Free Member shares your post to another Social Site and somebody who has never visited the site before clicks and makes a purchase that Free Member receives a 5% Lifetime Commission on any purchases made by their referral. Thereby creating a built-in Viral Marketing System.
